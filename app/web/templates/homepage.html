What the workflow actually looks like
A calmer cycle from search to sent folder.

Start by searching for labs and faculty whose work you can genuinely reference. Save the strongest matches, keep notes on what they study, then generate drafts only when your own background and the professor record are both ready.

After that, review the copy like an editor would: check whether the hook is specific, whether the ask is realistic, and whether the message sounds like a student who actually read the lab page or recent paper list.
