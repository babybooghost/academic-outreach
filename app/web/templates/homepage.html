How It Works

Five steps to your first outreach

1

Find Professors

Use the Finder to search by research topic. Filter by Ivy League, Top CS, Texas schools, UC system, or any of 100+ universities.

2

Enrich & Summarize

The system fetches research papers, extracts keywords, and builds a profile of each professor's work — so your emails reference real research.

3

Generate Drafts

Personalized emails generated from templates with built-in variation. Each gets quality scored and checked for genericness.

4

Review & Approve

Read every email, edit if needed, approve the good ones, reject the rest. You're always in control.

5

Send as Gmail Drafts

Approved emails become Gmail drafts. Review one final time in Gmail, then hit send when ready.
